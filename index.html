How to Use Class Points

1. Add a Student: Click on the "Enter student name" field, type a name, and press Enter or click "Add Student."

2. Update Points: Click the ❤️, +5, or +10 buttons next to a student’s name to increase their points. You can also enter a number manually.

3. Bulk Update: Select multiple students by clicking their avatars or names, then use the bulk +1, +5, or +10 buttons at the bottom to update all selected students.

4. Select/Deselect All: Use the "🔘 Select..." button to quickly toggle selection for all students.

5. Reset Points: Click "☰" then "💣 Reset Points" to set all selected students' scores back to zero. If no students are selected, it resets all students.

6. Sort Students: Use "↕️ Sort" then "🔠 by Name" to arrange students alphabetically or "↕️ Sort" then "🏅 by Score" to order them by points.

7. Customize Avatars: Click the settings icon (three dots) on a student’s card to modify their avatar features, including face shape, accessories, and colors.

8. Backup & Restore: Click "☰" then "⬆️ Backup" to download student data as a CSV file. Click "☰" then "⬇️ Restore" to restore data from a CSV file.

9. Undo: Click "↩️ Undo" to revert the last action (supports up to 10 undo steps).

10. Celebrate: Click "🎉 Celebrate!" to launch confetti and recognize achievements!

11. Class Photo: Click "☰" then "📸 Class Photo" to enter class photo mode. Print with no margin and backgrounds enabled for best results. Click the "X" to exit.
